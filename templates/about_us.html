About AgriSense

Transforming Agriculture with Artificial Intelligence

AgriSense is a comprehensive agricultural intelligence platform that leverages machine learning, computer vision, and data analytics to empower farmers, traders, and agricultural stakeholders in Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

To democratize access to agricultural intelligence, enabling data-driven decision making that increases productivity, profitability, and sustainability across Sri Lanka's agricultural sector.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's leading agricultural AI platform, transforming traditional farming practices and creating a more resilient and profitable agricultural ecosystem for future generations.

What We Do

Price Prediction

Accurate market price forecasts using ML models

Market Analysis

Optimal market selection with cost analysis

Cultivation Planning

AI-powered crop recommendations

Quality Assessment

Crop quality analysis using computer vision

Our Journey

2023 - Concept & Research

Initial research and market analysis identified the need for agricultural AI solutions in Sri Lanka.

2024 Q1 - Development Begins

Started developing the core ML models and web application framework.

2024 Q2 - Beta Testing

Conducted pilot testing with farmers and traders in selected regions.

2024 Q3 - Platform Launch

Launched AgriSense with four core modules for public use.

2024 Q4 - Expansion

Added mobile support and expanded to cover all agricultural regions.
